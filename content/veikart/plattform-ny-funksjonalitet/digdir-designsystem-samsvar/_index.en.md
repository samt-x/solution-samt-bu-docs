SAMT-BU Docs is built on a theme based on Altinn Docdock / the older Altinn design system. Digdir maintains an active, updated design system used across public services. It should be investigated to what extent SAMT-BU Docs should align with this.

## Questions to clarify

- What is Digdir's current design system, and is there an open component library?
- To what extent are fonts, colours, and layout in SAMT-BU Docs aligned with or in conflict with Digdir guidelines?
- Is it desirable to adapt to the Digdir system, or is it sufficient to have a consistent style of our own?
- Are there Hugo-compatible resources or reference implementations available?
